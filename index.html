Meet the Founder

Care Led with Intention

Domira Home Care was built to provide a more consistent, well-managed approach to care at home—one grounded in clear standards, thoughtful matching, and ongoing oversight.

Founded by Nick Julian, a nurse with experience across home health, hospice, and post-acute care operations, Domira reflects both clinical understanding and operational discipline. Care is approached with structure from the beginning, with attention to caregiver selection, communication, and early follow-up to ensure the right fit.

The result is a more stable, more dependable care experience—where families can feel confident and supported from the start.

A More Thoughtful Approach to Care

Care at home works best when it is guided with intention from the very beginning. At Domira, we focus on consistency, clear communication, and proactive oversight to ensure care is not only in place, but working as intended. From the initial assessment through ongoing support, we take a structured approach to caregiver matching, coordination, and follow-up—so there is less uncertainty, caregivers are set up for success, and care remains attentive, personalized, and stable over time.

Caregiver Matching That Fits

Care begins with the right fit. We carefully match caregivers based on skillset, personality, and the specific needs of the client and home environment. When the fit is right, routines develop more naturally and care feels more comfortable from the start.

Higher Caregiver Standards

We take a deliberate approach to caregiver selection, focusing on experience, reliability, and professionalism. This helps ensure that every caregiver who enters the home is prepared, engaged, and aligned with the level of care families expect.

Early and Ongoing Communication

The early stages of care set the tone for everything that follows. We maintain communication with both clients and caregivers to ensure expectations are aligned, routines are working well, and any adjustments can be made early.

Proactive Oversight

Care is guided with attention and awareness. By staying engaged and attentive to how care is progressing, we support both clients and caregivers in a way that keeps everything running smoothly and consistently.

Consistency You Can Rely On

Whenever possible, we prioritize caregiver consistency. Familiarity builds trust, creates comfort, and allows caregivers to better understand preferences, routines, and subtle changes over time.
